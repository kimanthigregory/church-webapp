The Solemnity of Corpus Christi: A Public Witness of Love

Prepare for our annual Eucharistic Procession through the streets of Kiserian. This ancient tradition serves as a profound public witness of our faith in the Real Presence of Christ. Join the community in song and prayer as we follow the King of Kings.
